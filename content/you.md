for once
I’m not trying to fill 
the void I carry

I was losing myself in darkness
ready to face the storm

but you came
a ray of light, to wash 
my tired face

my thoughts carry your name
constantly
I will write for you
again, but in the end
no words can describe 
what I feel for you

déjà vu
